Welcome to the Universe

Astronomy is the scientific study of celestial objects, such as stars, planets, galaxies, and other phenomena that originate outside the Earth's atmosphere. It is a fascinating field that has helped us learn more about our place in the universe and the origins of life.

Amazing Facts About the Universe

Here are some mind-blowing facts about the universe that will make you appreciate just how incredible it really is:

  • The universe is estimated to be about 13.8 billion years old.
  • The largest known star is 1,700 times bigger than the sun.
  • There are more stars in the universe than there are grains of sand on all the beaches on Earth.
  • Black holes are regions of space where the gravitational pull is so strong that nothing, not even light, can escape.
  • There may be as many as 500 billion planets in our galaxy alone.
